Bespoke Software Development

We are able to provide a range of software development services for small-medium size projects.  Each project will be customized to meet your requirements and can benefit from ongoing support.

Legacy Systems

vLife Systems specializes in the maintenance, extension and re-writing of legacy software.  In addition we have facilities for custom data conversion and migration so that customers can move their data from older systems to newer ones.

New Projects

vLife Systems is currently undergoing an intense period of Research and Development and is therefore not taking on any new projects.  We hope to be available by the end of May 2010.

Open Source

We are commited to using and creating the highest quality open source software.  This means that the tools used for your project are freely available and therefore any continuing development on your project doesn't require buying expensive proprietary software and therefore limiting your choice of developers.  It also ensures that while the software written is tailored to your specific needs, you will also benefit from continued development on the software that your project is based on.
